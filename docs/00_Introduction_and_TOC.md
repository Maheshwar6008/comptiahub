## 🎯 CompTIA A+ Kya Hai?

**CompTIA A+** duniya ki sabse respected **entry-level IT certification** hai.

Socho aise — jaise driving license ke bina gaadi nahi chala sakte, waise hi IT industry mein kaam karne ke liye **A+ certification** ek tarah ka "IT License" hai.

### Yeh Certification Kaun Deta Hai?
- **CompTIA** (Computing Technology Industry Association) — yeh ek American organization hai jo IT certifications deti hai
- **Globally recognized** — India, US, UK, Middle East — har jagah valid hai

### A+ Certification Se Kya Jobs Milti Hain?
| Job Role | Average Salary (India) | Kya Karte Hain? |
|---|---|---|
| IT Support Technician | ₹2.5 - 5 LPA | Computer problems solve karte hain |
| Help Desk Analyst | ₹3 - 6 LPA | Phone/chat pe users ki help karte hain |
| Desktop Support Engineer | ₹3 - 7 LPA | Office mein computers maintain karte hain |
| Field Service Technician | ₹3 - 6 LPA | Client locations pe jaake fix karte hain |
| IT Administrator (Junior) | ₹4 - 8 LPA | Office ka poora IT system dekhte hain |

### V15 Mein Kya Naya Hai?
- **AI in IT Support** — Artificial Intelligence kaise IT mein use hota hai
- **6GHz Wi-Fi (Wi-Fi 6E/7)** — Latest wireless technology
- **Advanced Cloud Computing** — Hybrid work environments
- **Updated Security** — Modern cyber threats aur unse protection
- **Sustainability** — Green IT practices
